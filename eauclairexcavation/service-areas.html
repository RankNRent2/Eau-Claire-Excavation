Cities & Communities We Serve

Our excavation crews operate throughout the Chippewa Valley. Below are the communities we most regularly serve — but we travel farther for the right project. Call us and ask.

Eau Claire County

  • Eau Claire — our home base; city and county seat. All excavation services available.
  • Altoona — directly east of Eau Claire; growing residential area with active new construction.
  • Fall Creek — rural residential and agricultural excavation work.
  • Elk Mound — village and surrounding town parcels.
  • Augusta — southern Eau Claire County.
  • Fairchild — rural and agricultural projects.

Chippewa County

  • Chippewa Falls — residential, commercial, and industrial excavation.
  • Bloomer — residential and agricultural projects.
  • Stanley — growing community in northern Chippewa County.
  • Colfax — village center and surrounding rural properties.
  • Cornell — residential and farm projects.
  • Lake Hallie — suburban residential area near Chippewa Falls.

Dunn County & Beyond

  • Menomonie — UW-Stout area; residential and commercial projects.
  • Boyceville — rural western Dunn County.
  • Mondovi — Buffalo County border area.
  • Osseo — Trempealeau County, northeast of Eau Claire.
  • Durand — Pepin County, along the Chippewa River.
  • Other areas — call us; we travel for larger projects.
