What Site Preparation Includes

Before any structure goes up, the land has to be ready. Site preparation is the first and most critical step in any construction project — and getting it right means your foundation, utilities, and drainage will perform correctly for decades.

Our Eau Claire site prep crew handles every phase of the clearing and grading process:

  • Vegetation clearing — Removal of trees, brush, stumps, and organic matter from the build area
  • Grubbing — Stripping topsoil and root systems to expose stable subsoil for your foundation
  • Rough grading — Shaping the lot so water drains away from the structure in all directions
  • Soil compaction — Preparing a stable base for slab, footings, or crawl space construction
  • Cut and fill — Moving soil on-site or importing/exporting fill to achieve the right elevations
  • Debris hauling — Removing all cleared material so your builder starts with a clean site

What Does Site Preparation Cost in Eau Claire?

Site prep costs in the Eau Claire area typically range from $1,500 to $8,000+ depending on lot size, tree density, slope, and soil conditions. A flat half-acre residential lot with moderate vegetation usually runs $2,000–$4,000. Heavily wooded or sloped lots cost more. The only way to know for sure is an on-site visit — which we provide free of charge.
