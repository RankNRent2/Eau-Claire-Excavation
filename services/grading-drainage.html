Grading & Drainage in Eau Claire, WI

Lot grading, French drain installation, swales, and erosion control to keep water moving away from your home and your property staying dry throughout Wisconsin's wet seasons.

Fix Drainage Problems Before They Damage Your Home

Wisconsin's freeze-thaw cycles, spring snowmelt, and heavy summer rains put real stress on improperly graded lots. Water pooling near a foundation, eroding hillsides, and soggy yards are all solvable problems — and the solution almost always starts with proper grading.

Grading Services

  • Final grading — shaping soil around new construction to achieve positive drainage away from the foundation on all sides
  • Regrading existing lots — fixing negative grade situations where water flows toward the house
  • Driveway grading — establishing correct slope for new and existing gravel or paved driveways
  • Yard leveling — filling low spots and creating even, usable outdoor space

Drainage Solutions

  • French drains — perforated pipe in gravel trenches to collect and redirect groundwater away from foundations and low areas
  • Surface swales — shaped channels that collect and redirect surface runoff
  • Catch basins & yard drains — collecting standing water in low points and routing it to safe discharge
  • Erosion control — seeding, erosion blankets, and rip-rap placement on disturbed slopes
  • Culvert installation — corrugated pipe under driveways to maintain natural drainage channels

Grading & Drainage Cost in Eau Claire

Simple regrading of a typical residential lot in Eau Claire runs $500–$2,500. French drain installation ranges from $1,000 to $5,000+ depending on length and depth. Large drainage projects or catch-basin systems cost more. We provide free estimates on all drainage work.
