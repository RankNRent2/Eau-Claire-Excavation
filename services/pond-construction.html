Pond Construction in Eau Claire, WI

Residential backyard ponds, farm and livestock ponds, stormwater retention ponds, and recreational fishing ponds throughout Chippewa County and the Eau Claire area.

Pond Excavation for Chippewa Valley Properties

Wisconsin is pond country — and the Chippewa Valley's mix of clay soils, rolling terrain, and abundant water table makes it ideal for building new ponds that hold water reliably year-round. Whether you want a backyard water feature, a farm pond for livestock, a recreational fishing pond, or a stormwater retention basin, we have the equipment and experience to build it right.

Types of Ponds We Build

  • Residential backyard ponds — decorative water features and small recreational ponds for Eau Claire area homeowners
  • Farm and livestock ponds — water sources for cattle, horses, and other livestock on Chippewa County farms
  • Recreational fishing ponds — stocked fishing ponds for rural properties, with proper depth profiles and inlet/outlet structures
  • Stormwater retention ponds — engineered basins that capture runoff from commercial and residential developments
  • Fire suppression ponds — water storage for rural properties and facilities without municipal water access
  • Irrigation ponds — water storage for agricultural irrigation systems

What Pond Construction Involves

Building a pond that holds water and stays structurally sound over decades requires more than just digging a hole. Our process includes:

  • Site assessment — evaluating soil permeability, groundwater depth, drainage area, and topography
  • Excavation — digging to the correct depth and shape based on pond type and desired water volume
  • Compaction and sealing — compacting clay soils in the basin walls and bottom to hold water; bentonite clay lining where soils are sandy
  • Inlet and outlet structures — installing overflow pipes and spillways to manage water level and protect the embankment
  • Embankment construction — building and compacting earth berms around the pond perimeter
  • Spoil management — grading excavated material on-site or hauling off

Wisconsin DNR Pond Permits

Many pond projects in Wisconsin require a permit from the Wisconsin DNR — particularly those involving waterway crossings, wetland areas, or ponds larger than certain thresholds. We're familiar with Chippewa County's permitting requirements and can advise you on what your project will need before we start digging.

Pond Construction Cost in Eau Claire

A small residential pond (under 1/4 acre) typically runs $3,000–$8,000. Farm ponds of 1/2 to 1 acre generally cost $8,000–$20,000 depending on depth, soil conditions, and structures required. Larger retention and irrigation ponds are quoted on a project basis. Call us for a free on-site assessment.
