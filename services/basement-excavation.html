Basement Excavation in Eau Claire, WI

New basement digging, crawl space excavation, and walkout basement construction for homes and additions throughout Eau Claire County and Chippewa County.

New Basement Digging for Eau Claire Homes

Adding a basement to new construction or underpinning an existing foundation is precision work. Wisconsin soils — from the sandy loams near the Chippewa River to the heavier clay soils in parts of Chippewa County — require experience and the right equipment to excavate safely and to spec.

We handle:

  • Full basement excavation for new home construction — working from your foundation plans to dig to the correct depth and footprint
  • Walkout basement digging — accounting for slope, drainage, and egress door placement
  • Crawl space excavation — for homes where a full basement isn't planned but access and vapor barrier installation require some digging
  • Garage basement/pit digging — storage pits, car lifts, and workshop spaces below garage slabs
  • Excavation for additions — expanding an existing home's footprint and tying new foundation into existing structure
  • Soil hauling and disposal — removing excavated material from site or stockpiling for backfill

Working With Your Contractor

Basement excavation is one of the first things that happens on a construction site. We coordinate directly with your general contractor and foundation crew to ensure the dig is on time, to depth, and ready for footings. We provide a clean, safe excavation and stay out of the way once the foundation contractor takes over.

Basement Excavation Cost in Eau Claire

A typical full basement excavation for a new home in the Eau Claire area runs $3,000 to $8,000 depending on soil conditions, basement size, depth, access, and how far excavated material needs to travel. Walkouts and difficult access situations add cost. Call us for a free on-site estimate before you budget.

Permits & Wisconsin Dig Safe

All basement excavations require a Wisconsin Dig Safe (Digger's Hotline) call before any digging begins — we handle this on your behalf as part of every project. If your project requires a grading or excavation permit from the City of Eau Claire or Chippewa County, we can assist with the process.
